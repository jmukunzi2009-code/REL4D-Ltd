About REL4D Hub

A registered consultancy firm specializing in research, evaluations, and learning initiatives for international development programs.

Registered Consultancy

Transforming Development Through Evidence-Based Approaches

REL4D Hub Ltd is a registered consultancy firm (TIN: 121718400) based in Kigali, Rwanda, specializing in conducting research, evaluations, and supporting learning initiatives for international development programs.

We leverage a blend of traditional and computational methods to provide customized evaluations, research, data analysis, interactive visualizations, predictive modeling, and learning strategies that bridge evidence with practice across the program lifecycle.

  • End-to-end research and evaluation services
  • Cutting-edge data analytics and machine learning
  • Monitoring, evaluation, and learning (MEL) systems
  • Capacity building and knowledge management

Our Services

  • Theory of Change Development
  • Results Framework Design
  • Custom Indicator Development
  • M&E Plan Creation

System Customization

  • Analytics Dashboards
  • AI-Powered Insights
  • Real-time Reporting

Research & Evaluation

  • Market Analysis
  • Learning Assessments
  • Predictive Analytics

Capacity Building
